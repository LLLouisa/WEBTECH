MIS3690 - Project Proposal - Xialu (Louisa) ZHONG

What is your website about?

The website contains introduction and analysis of authentic Chinese hotpot culture (especially in Chongqing region), and recommended hotpot restaurants in Boston area.

What is the objective of your website?

The objective of this website is to increase overall awareness of Chinese hotpot, and to bring Chinese culture internationally.

What is your target audience set?

My target audiences includes:

  • people who are interested in food and cuisine;
  • people who love spicy food;
  • people who live in Boston area.

What content do you plan to include in your website?

  • Homepage;
  • Origin and history;
  • Benefits of eating hotpot;
  • The process of preparing and eating hotpot;
  • Recommended hotpot restaurants in Boston area.
