About Me

Babson College has ranked number one in entrepreneurship in the United States. When I visited Babson, the small class ratio, the alumni network and the Center for Career Development all intrigued me a lot. Upon graduation, I want to step in the management consulting industry. The experience of working as a part time assistant at Boston Consulting Group sparkled my curiosity to know more about strategic management. Consultants are always curious, talented and fast-learning, and they focus on a 360degree view to approach a certain problem. What makes me be more passionate about consulting is the fact that I am able to get new insights of different industries, to respond quickly to a new situation and to interact with people constantly.
