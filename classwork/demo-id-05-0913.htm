ID Class Demo

Professional behavior is expected.

Conduct yourself with colleagues and the professor as you would in a professional meeting with managers.

Arrive on time, don’t leave during the middle of the class, and be engaged in the discussion by actively participating and listening.

It is important to recognize that mere attendance does not constitute class participation.

Indulging in the usual computer- and internet-enabled distractions is inappropriate behavior and will negatively affect your grade.

You are welcome to seek your professor’s feedback about your performance on this component of the grade at any time during the semester.

The responsibility to do so is entirely yours.
