HISTORY


At the end of Qing dynasty and the beginning of Republic of China, along the Changjiang River, some should be disposed food like beef tripe, chicken feet and duck blood are sold to lower class people, especially boat tracker, who are not rich enough to buy meals. Then these tripes, lungs, intestines are cut into pieces, placing in different little plate, if someone wants to buy it, then place it into hot boiling water along with different spicy sauce and seasonings.
